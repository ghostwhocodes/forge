Read the request, typed architecture contract, typed decommission ledger, typed
test-governance artifact, and structured boundary findings artifacts.

Write a concise markdown summary to the `boundary_summary` artifact for human
curation. Prioritize the most important findings and for each one explain
whether it is:

- a direct contract break
- a lingering legacy path that should be removed
- or a test-governance ambiguity that is still pulling toward the old behavior

Structure the summary as:

1. **Overview** — one-paragraph situation summary
2. **Findings** — numbered list, one per finding from `boundary_findings`,
   with its id, category, and a short plain-language explanation
3. **Recommendation** — a clear suggestion of fix, rescan, or complete, with
   reasoning

Make the next human decision easy: set `finding_selection_status` to `fix`
(proceed to guardrail remediation), `rescan` (re-run drift detection), or
`complete` (no changes needed).
