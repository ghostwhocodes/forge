Read the request artifact, typed QA governance artifact, and structured
`qa_gap_findings` artifact.

Write a concise markdown summary to the `qa_gap_summary` artifact for human
curation.

Structure the summary as:

1. **Overview** — one paragraph on the current QA confidence posture
2. **Priority gaps** — numbered list covering each finding id, category,
   severity, and plain-language explanation of why it still matters
3. **Recommended next step** — say whether the operator should package gaps,
   rescan, or complete, and why

Keep the summary focused on meaningful confidence gaps and avoid turning it
into a file inventory.

Make the next human decision easy: set `gap_selection_status` to `accepted`
(package selected QA gaps), `rescan` (rerun detection), or `complete` (no
remediation package needed).
